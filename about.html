* About *


Lucia Kan-Sperling is from Northampton, Massachusetts.

She recently completed her B.A. at Brown University, where she studied Modern Culture & Media and English.

She has interned at DT Jung (formerly Junges DT) @ Deutsches Theater Berlin, the Thessaloniki International Film Festival, and Talkhouse Media. She has also worked as directorial assistant, digital content director, web designer, and social media manager for theater productions at Junges DT and the Theaterdiscounter in Berlin.

She is currently based in Taipei, Taiwan.
